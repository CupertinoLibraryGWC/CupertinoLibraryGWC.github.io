About Us

We are the members from a club called the Girls Who Code, a national non-profit organization dedicated to closing the gender gap in technology. During the past 6 months, we've been meeting at Cupertino Library to make a website about dealing with stress. Ranging from middle-schoolers to high-schoolers, we learned how to code for our website and how to work as a team to complete a website, even though some of us have no prior experience. From Girls Who Code, we were able to engage ourselves with technology to help create a potential future as a software engineer. We hope you enjoy our website as we volunteered our two hours every week to create something impressive from scratch.
